# Sample Financial Policy Document

## Investment Advisory Agreement

### 1. Investment Management Services

The Advisor agrees to provide investment management services to the Client based on the Client's investment objectives, risk tolerance, and financial circumstances. The Advisor will:

- Monitor the Client's portfolio on a continuous basis
- Make investment decisions based on the Client's risk profile
- Provide regular performance reports
- Rebalance the portfolio as needed

### 2. Risk Disclosure

**IMPORTANT**: All investments involve risk of loss. Past performance does not guarantee future results. The Client should be prepared to bear the risk of loss of principal.

Types of risks include:
- Market risk from economic conditions
- Credit risk from issuer default
- Liquidity risk from inability to sell
- Interest rate risk affecting bond values
- Inflation risk reducing purchasing power

### 3. Fees and Compensation

The Advisor charges an annual management fee of 1.25% of assets under management, calculated quarterly. Additional fees may apply for:
- Trading commissions
- Custodian fees
- Fund expense ratios

### 4. Client Responsibilities

Clients must:
- Provide accurate financial information
- Notify the Advisor of changes in financial situation
- Review account statements regularly
- Understand the risks associated with their investments

### 5. Regulatory Compliance

This agreement is subject to all applicable federal and state securities laws. The Advisor is registered with the SEC and follows all fiduciary standards.

### 6. Termination

Either party may terminate this agreement with 30 days written notice. Upon termination, the Client will pay all fees earned through the termination date.
